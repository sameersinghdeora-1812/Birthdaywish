A Letter, Just For You

Dear you,

I've been trying to figure out how to write this for a while now. But I think I've been overthinking it — because with you, words have never really been the point. We understand each other without them, remember?

But today is your birthday. Your 19th. And I want you to know what that means to me.

It means 17 years of knowing you. 17 years of finding my person over and over again in you — through every phase, every change, every version of who we were becoming. You were there when I was just a small, chaotic little kid, and you're here now, as I'm becoming whoever I'm becoming.

You are genuinely one of the most beautiful humans I've ever known. Not just because you're pretty — though you are, obviously — but because of the way you carry yourself. Strong. Brave. Present. The way you can make someone feel completely seen without even trying.

I think about how crazy it is that this all started at age 2. Tu meri first friend thi, and somehow the universe just decided — yeah, these two. These two are going to be something.

Different universities now. Different cities. But every single video call, every late-night message, every "goodnight, sweet horror dreams" just proves — what we have doesn't need geography. It just needs us.

I hope this year — your 19th — is the most beautiful one yet. I hope it's full of laughter, breakthroughs, and moments that make you feel completely alive.

Happy birthday, my person. You deserve the whole universe. 🌸

— Always yours, from age 2 💖
